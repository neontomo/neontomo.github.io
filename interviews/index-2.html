Hi Yamudazu! Nice to meet you.

I want to start by asking you some easy questions and then we move to the juicy stuff…

For someone who knows nothing about you, can you describe Yamudazu in one sentence?

Great, thank you. And how long have you been making NFTs? Did you always make art?

And what's your favourite food?

Recently you’ve been sharing your past traumas in your art, are you okay talking about that?

And were you always comfortable telling your story?

How did those experiences shape you into the individual you are today? What choices have you made?

How did you learn to navigate the NFT space, did you have any teachers? What did that process look like for you?

You are a very giving creator, boosting other artists, offering regular feedback and giving more than you take. Where does that generosity come from?

I can see a lot of emotions in your art, what deeper meaning does art have for you?

What is your relationship with society? Do you ever struggle with "normal" life?

What is something you love that most people don’t pay any attention to?

Tell us about your personal philosophy. What matters to Yamudazu?

You have a lot of colours in your art, is there a reason for that artistic choice?

Thank you so much for opening up to me. One last question before we finish the interview.

You’ve gone through an incredible journey to become who you are today, do you think young Yamudazu would have believed it? What advice would you give to him?
